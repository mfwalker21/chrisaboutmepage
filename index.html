About Chris

Chris Camacho is an ambivert who is passionate about learning new skills, driven by curiosity, and values collaboration and sharing knowledge to serve the community.

Chris, a proud Bronx native, finds inspiration in the rich cultural history of his hometown and known for its amazing food and vibrant Black-owned businesses. Passionate about coding, Chris enjoys creating web designs that blend minimalist and bold aesthetics. With a background in computer science and creative nonfiction writing, Chris aspires to pursue a career in interactive web design, UX/UI, and psychology to study the user interactions.
